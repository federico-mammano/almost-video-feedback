How it works

Four steps. Most of it is just talking.

Record

Click the icon or hit a hotkey.

Talk & point

Describe bugs while you click and scroll.

Stop

The bundle copies to your clipboard.

Paste & run

Drop it into your AI agent. Done.

Get started

Installed in a minute.

  1. Download the .zip and unzip it somewhere permanent.
  2. Open chrome://extensions → turn on Developer mode.
  3. Click Load unpacked → pick the folder. Pin it.
